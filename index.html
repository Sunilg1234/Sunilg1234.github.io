Acer Predator Helios 300

Introduction

Acer Predator Helios 300 is a budget gaming laptop from Acer. Altogether it has seven models :-

  1. With i5 8thgen cpu , 8gb ram, Nvidia GTX 1050ti gpu , 60hz screen
  2. With i5 8thgen cpu , 16gb ram, Nvidia GTX 1050ti gpu , 60hz screen
  3. With i7 8thgen cpu , 8gb ram, Nvidia GTX 1060 gpu 6gb vram , 60hz screen
  4. With i7 8thgen cpu , 8gb ram, Nvidia GTX 1060 gpu 6gb vram , 144hz screen
  5. With i7 8thgen cpu , 16gb ram, Nvidia GTX 1060 gpu 6gb vram, 60hz screen
  6. With i7 8thgen cpu , 16gb ram, Nvidia GTX 1060 gpu 6gb vram , 144hz screen

Because of many complaints with its screen in previous years , In 2018 Acer added version with 144hz screen .
